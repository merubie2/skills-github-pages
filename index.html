What is Zenless Zone Zero (ZZZ)?

ZZZ™ is a free-to-play action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo (with publishing outside mainland China under Cognosphere, HoYoverse). The game was released on Windows, iOS, Android & PlayStation 5 on July 4, 2024.
